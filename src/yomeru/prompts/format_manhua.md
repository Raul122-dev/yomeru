# Manhua (Chinese) — Reading format

## Reading direction: LEFT to RIGHT, TOP to BOTTOM (mainland China standard)

Traditional/Hong Kong manhua may be right-to-left — if you see vertical traditional Chinese text, treat as RTL like manga.

### Panel numbering

Standard LTR: top-left panel = 0, proceed left→right, then down.

### Chinese bubble conventions

- Narration: rectangular boxes, often with decorative borders
- SFX: stylized Chinese characters integrated into art
- Thought bubbles: typically cloud-shaped
- Full-color production is common in modern manhua

### Simplified vs Traditional

- Simplified Chinese (mainland): 爱 not 愛
- Traditional Chinese (Taiwan/HK): 愛 not 爱
  Note which variant is used — this affects reading direction assumption.
